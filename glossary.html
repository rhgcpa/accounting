Accrual Basis
Accounting method that records revenues and expenses when they are incurred, not when cash is exchanged. [Topic 2.7.2]
Asset
Present economic resources, over which the entity has a present right, a potential benefit, and an exclusive control, as a result of past events. [Topic 6.1]
Liability
Present obligation of the entity arising from past events, settlement of which is expected to result in an outflow of resources. [Topic 6.2]
Equity
Residual interest in the assets of the entity after deducting liabilities. [Topic 6.3]
Revenue
Income arising in the course of ordinary activities of an entity. [Topic 6.4]
